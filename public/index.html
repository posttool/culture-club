Welcome to the experiment. This project exists to explore the idea of knowledge in the context of dialog. The idea is that agents can be defined and participate in a discussion. Agents can acknowledge or reject other agent's perspectives within the culture. The hope is that the agent can use the feedback to optimize its performance (to adhere to the values of the culture).
